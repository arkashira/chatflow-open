# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for chatflow-open

### 1. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Enables users to connect chatflow-open with thousands of other apps, automating workflows and enhancing productivity.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Automates user job of managing customer interactions across multiple platforms, saving time and reducing manual effort.

### 2. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Facilitates payment processing for any premium features or services offered through chatflow-open.
   - **Free-tier limits**: No monthly fees, 2.9% + 30¢ per successful card charge.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Solves the user job of monetizing customer support interactions, allowing for seamless payment collection.

### 3. **Slack**
   - **Rationale**: Allows integration of chatflow-open notifications and interactions directly into Slack channels for real-time updates.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 10 integrations, 5GB file storage.
   - **Integration effort**: Small (S).
   - **Value-add**: Enhances team collaboration by keeping support teams informed and engaged without switching platforms.

### 4. **Google Analytics**
   - **Rationale**: Provides insights into user interactions and engagement metrics within the chat platform.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Up to 10 million hits per month.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Helps users track performance and optimize customer support strategies based on data-driven insights.

### 5. **Mailchimp**
   - **Rationale**: Enables users to manage email marketing campaigns based on chat interactions and customer feedback.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Solves the user job of nurturing leads and maintaining customer relationships through targeted email campaigns.

### 6. **Twilio**
   - **Rationale**: Allows integration of SMS and voice capabilities into chatflow-open for enhanced customer communication.
   - **Free-tier limits**: $15 credit for new accounts.
   - **Integration effort**: Large (L).
   - **Value-add**: Expands communication channels, addressing user needs for multi-channel support.

### 7. **Calendly**
   - **Rationale**: Simplifies scheduling meetings directly from chat interactions, improving customer support efficiency.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1 calendar type, unlimited scheduled events.
   - **Integration effort**: Small (S).
   - **Value-add**: Solves the user job of scheduling follow-up meetings without back-and-forth emails.

### 8. **HubSpot**
   - **Rationale**: Integrates CRM functionalities to manage customer relationships and track interactions from chatflow-open.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1 million contacts, limited features.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium (M).
   - **Value-add**: Addresses the user job of maintaining comprehensive customer profiles and improving follow-up strategies.

---

This roadmap prioritizes integrations that enhance the functionality of chatflow-open while providing opportunities for affiliate or revenue-sharing models where applicable.
