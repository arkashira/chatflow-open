# Customer Journey Map for chatflow-open

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: A business experiences dissatisfaction with their current customer support solution (e.g., high costs, lack of customization).
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty in finding alternatives; overwhelming number of options; unclear benefits of self-hosted solutions.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, hope for a better solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide clear, concise comparison charts showing benefits of self-hosting vs. SaaS; offer educational content about the advantages of open-source solutions.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits, engagement with educational content.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: The business starts researching alternatives to their current solution.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about the complexity of self-hosting; fear of losing features available in SaaS platforms; uncertainty about support and updates.
- **User Emotions**: Skepticism, cautious optimism, curiosity.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer detailed documentation and case studies showcasing successful implementations; provide a comparison of features with popular SaaS solutions.
- **Metric**: Time spent on product pages, downloads of documentation.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: The business decides to test chatflow-open by setting it up in a sandbox environment.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup challenges; potential technical issues; lack of immediate support.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety; apprehension about technical hurdles.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide a guided setup wizard; offer live chat support during the trial period; create a community forum for peer support.
- **Metric**: Number of installations, user feedback during trial.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: The business successfully implements chatflow-open and begins using it in their operations.
- **Friction Points**: Learning curve for staff; integration with existing tools; ensuring all features are utilized.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction if successful; frustration if issues arise.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized onboarding sessions; provide training resources and webinars; highlight advanced features that can enhance their use.
- **Metric**: User engagement metrics, feature usage statistics.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: The business sees positive results and considers expanding the use of chatflow-open (e.g., adding more users, integrating with other systems).
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about scaling; potential costs associated with expansion; need for additional features.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence, enthusiasm, but also apprehension about the future.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Showcase success stories of other businesses that scaled successfully; offer incentives for expanding usage (e.g., discounts for additional users); provide a roadmap for future features based on user feedback.
- **Metric**: Number of new users added, revenue growth from expanded usage.
