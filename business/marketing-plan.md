# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Chatflow-Open: Your self-hostable customer support chat platform, empowering businesses with flexibility and control without vendor lock-in."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. "Empower your support team with Chatflow-Open, the open-source chat solution that adapts to your needs."
2. "Say goodbye to vendor lock-in with Chatflow-Open, the customizable chat platform for modern customer support."
3. "Chatflow-Open: The freedom to support your customers on your terms, with a self-hosted solution that scales with your business."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Sarah, Customer Support Manager**
   - **Daily Job:** Overseeing the customer support team and ensuring timely responses to customer inquiries.
   - **Budget:** $500/month for tools to enhance customer support efficiency.

2. **Persona 2: Mark, CTO of a Mid-sized Tech Company**
   - **Daily Job:** Evaluating and implementing technology solutions that improve operational efficiency while maintaining data security.
   - **Budget:** $1,500/month for software solutions that can be self-hosted and customized.

3. **Persona 3: Lisa, Founder of a Start-up**
   - **Daily Job:** Managing all aspects of the business, including customer support, marketing, and product development.
   - **Budget:** $300/month for essential tools that help streamline customer interactions.

## Channels
1. **Channel 1: Content Marketing (Blog Posts, Case Studies)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 per customer (based on content production and promotion costs).

2. **Channel 2: Developer Communities (Forums, GitHub)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $150 per customer (leveraging organic reach and community engagement).

3. **Channel 3: Social Media Advertising (LinkedIn, Twitter)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $250 per customer (targeted ads focused on tech decision-makers).

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing Chatflow-Open and its benefits.
- **Week 2:** Case study on a successful implementation of Chatflow-Open.
- **Week 3:** How-to guide on setting up Chatflow-Open for your business.
- **Week 4:** Webinar on the advantages of self-hosted solutions vs. SaaS.
- **Week 5:** User testimonials and feedback compilation.
- **Week 6:** Blog post on best practices for customer support using Chatflow-Open.
- **Week 7:** Infographic comparing Chatflow-Open with traditional SaaS solutions.
- **Week 8:** Q&A session on social media addressing common questions about Chatflow-Open.
- **Week 9:** Release of a video tutorial series on using Chatflow-Open.
- **Week 10:** Blog post on future updates and roadmap for Chatflow-Open.
- **Week 11:** Community engagement post encouraging user feedback and feature requests.
- **Week 12:** Recap of the launch and user experiences shared.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and prepare marketing materials.
- **D-0:** Official launch of Chatflow-Open with press release and promotional activities.
- **D+30:** Evaluate initial user feedback and make necessary adjustments; launch follow-up content.
- **D+90:** Review performance metrics and plan for future enhancements based on user input.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Target of 1,000 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) Target:** $10,000 by D+90, based on subscription model and user acquisition strategies.
